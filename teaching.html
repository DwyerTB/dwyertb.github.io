Teaching

During my undergraduate, I gained extensive experience as a teaching assistant in computer science. As an instructor, it is extremely important to me to foster an inclusive environment, especially in STEM fields. My work to this end led to my nomination as a Teaching Assistant Trainer in Penn's School of Engineering, where I trained new teaching assistants in practices of inclusive teaching and student support.

Teaching Assistantships: Computer Science

CIS 380: Operating Systems, University of Pennsylvania. Primary Instructor: Dr. Boon Thau Loo. Fall 2021.

Held office hours, graded student projects and tests, and provided mentorship for several student groups completing the final project of developing a basic operating system.

CIS 240: Introduction to Computer Architecture, University of Pennsylvania. Primary Instructor: Dr. Thomas Farmer. Spring 2021.

Held office hours, led recitations and review sessions, and graded student projects and tests.

NETS 212: Scalable and Cloud Computing, University of Pennsylvania. Primary Instructor: Dr. Andreas Haeberlen. Fall 2020.

Held office hours, graded student projects and tests, and help develop course materials as the course was restructured into a project elective.

CIS 192: Intro to Python, University of Pennsylvania. Primary Instructor: Arun Kirubarajan. Fall 2019.

Held office hours and graded student projects.
